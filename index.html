Ubud

Days 1–6 · 5 nights · The cultural heart of Bali

🚚 Getting There

  • Airport to Ubud: Grab/Gojek $13–19 | or private transfer $25–30
  • Travel time: 1.5–2 hours (38 km)
  • Budget tip: Kura-Kura shuttle bus from Kuta $6/person (takes 3–4h)
  • Getting around: Scooter rental $3–5/day | Gojek rides $1–3

📷 Must-Visit in Ubud

Tegalalang Rice Terraces
Iconic layered paddies — go before 8am — free entry (small donation)
Campuhan Ridge Walk
Free sunrise walk through lush green valleys — best at 6am
Sacred Monkey Forest
$5 entry — playful monkeys in ancient temple setting
Tirta Empul Water Temple
$5 entry — join a water purification ceremony
Balinese Cooking Class
$35–50/person — market visit + cook together (Paon Bali is top-rated)
Tegenungan Waterfall
$1.50 entry — 15 min from Ubud, swimmable
Ubud Palace & Art Market
Free palace entry — market for souvenirs & handmade crafts
Couples Spa
90-min Balinese massage $8–12 — Ubud Traditional Massage

Nusa Penida

Days 7–9 · 3 nights · Wild cliffs & turquoise waters

🚢 Getting There & Around

  • Ubud → Sanur Port: Grab/Gojek $11–15 (1h)
  • Sanur → Nusa Penida (ferry): $7–12/person | 30–45 min
  • Nusa Penida → Seminyak (ferry+bus): $12–17/person | ~2.5h
  • On island: Scooter rental $5–8/day | or private driver $30–40/day

📷 Must-Visit on Nusa Penida

Kelingking Beach (T-Rex Cliff)
The iconic viewpoint — entry $0.70 — steep hike to beach (30–45 min down)
Angel's Billabong & Broken Beach
Natural infinity pool + dramatic archway — visit at low tide — free
Crystal Bay
Calm snorkeling with 300+ coral species — manta rays Jul–Nov
Diamond Beach & Atuh Beach
East coast gems — white sand, dramatic cliffs, fewer crowds
Snorkeling with Manta Rays
Manta Point tour $13–22/person — 2h trip, 70% sighting rate
Teletubbies Hill & Rumah Pohon
Rolling green hills + famous treehouse viewpoint

Days 10–14 · 5 nights · Sunsets, dining & relaxation

🚚 Getting There & Around

  • Nusa Penida → Seminyak: Combined ferry+bus ticket $12–17/person (2.5h)
  • Seminyak → Airport: Gojek/Grab $6–10 | 25–40 min (10 km)
  • Getting around: Walkable in central areas | Gojek $1–3
